Mac and Cheese

Cheesy Chewy Goodness

Making mac and cheese is a simple but tasty food to make.

The ingredients used are usually easily accesible so preparing a bowl is only a few steps away from being ready.

Ingredients

  • Macaroni
  • Butter
  • Milk
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Boil the macaroni
  2. Make the cheese sauce
  3. Bake the mac and cheese
