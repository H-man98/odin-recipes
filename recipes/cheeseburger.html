Cheeseburger

Simple Sandwich

A hamburger with cheese is a classic.

It doesn't require many ingredients to make a good burger so preparation should be trivial.

Ingredients

  • Hamburger Buns
  • Beef Patties
  • Seasoning
  • American Cheese
  • Condiments (i.e. ketchup, mayonnaise, mustard, etc)

Steps

  1. Thaw beef patty
  2. Season beef patty
  3. Cook beef patty
  4. Toast buns(optional)
  5. Put it all together
